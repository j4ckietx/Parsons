Pac-Bug

click on the canvas to start the game. Navigate with up/down/left/right keys.
(desktop only)

A MINI WEB GAME

The inspiration is taken from the T-Rex game in Chrome. The main goal of the design is to keep people occupied for a short period of time with nice color scheme and fun details, thus a "Game Over" ending is purposefully left out. The animation keeps going after click start till the webpage is refreshed.
The game is made with processing (java) and implemented onto the website.
