Challenge 100

Challenge 100 is a project I have decided to do with the intention of practicing the technical skills of design. The focus is mostly on graphic design and animation with an extensive use of different softwares including the Adobe Creative Suite and Cinema 4D. These are the technical skills I have the most experience in. And even though my current job as a junior designer for an online boutique doesn't require much advanced skills, I still intend to practices my observation and rendering skills of texture, composition, and lighting. Other new skills, practices, and advancements will be updated regularly in other blog posts.
