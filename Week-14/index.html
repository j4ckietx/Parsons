PROJECTS

The projects I have done in the past are categorized into three sections by different media. The first type is product designs, meaning that the projects completed are functional products created with study user experience and functional scenarios. The second type is graphics and motion graphics, meaning that the projects done are still images and animations created in digital or printed form in order to serve a visual purpose. The third type is experimentations, meaning that these projects showcased are completed projects with no intended goal or client, instead they are pure creation from the practice of new skills and aesthetic trends.
