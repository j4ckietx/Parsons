Bacterial Infestation

A microscopic view of abstract random movement of bacteria

A MODEL OF DIGITAL ANALOGY

The inspiration is from synbiotic relationship among different types of bacteria. Dfferent species of bacteria are usually living in the same environemnt without unnecessary competition. It has the highest efficiency of reproduction and sustainability in nature. This model created by java aims to mimic the visual effect of the bacterial life.
